About

This game is based on a fighting game called Injustice 2, where superman turns evil and Batman tries to stop him. This memory match game is a two player game, where players take turns matching cards to use to battle each other. The card value determines the battle results and the loser of a battle will lose a health point. Players have a total of three health points and the first to get the other player to zero health points wins!
